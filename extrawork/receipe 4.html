🍝 Creamy Pasta Recipe (White Sauce Style)

✅ Ingredients:

    For Pasta:
  • 2 cups pasta (any type: penne, macaroni, etc.)
  • Water + salt for boiling
    For White Sauce:
  • 2 tbsp butter or oil
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 cups milk (warm)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cheese (any, like cheddar or mozzarella)
    Optional Add-ins:
  • Boiled chicken or shredded cooked chicken
  • Vegetables (capsicum, corn, carrots, peas)
  • Garlic (finely chopped, 1 tsp)

    👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Boil the Pasta
    • In a pot, add pasta, water, and 1/2 tsp salt
    • Boil until pasta is soft but not mushy (8–10 mins)
    • Drain and set aside

  2. Make the White Sauce
    1. In a pan, melt butter
    2. Add flour and stir for 1 minute (don’t let it brown)
    3. Slowly add warm milk while stirring (to avoid lumps)
    4. Keep stirring until sauce thickens
    5. Add salt, black pepper, chili flakes
    6. Add cheese and mix until melted
  3. Mix Pasta with Sauce
    • Add boiled pasta to the sauce
    • Mix well so the pasta is fully coated
    • Add boiled chicken or veggies if using
    • Cook for 2–3 minutes and turn off heat

    🍽 Serve Hot With:

    • Garlic bread
    • Ketchup or chili sauce
    • prinkle more cheese or herbs on top
