🍫 Easy Chocolate Cake Recipe

✅ Ingredients:

    Dry:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
    Wet:
  • 1/2 cup oil or melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg (or 1/4 cup yogurt for eggless)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup hot water or coffee (adds richness)

    🧁 Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Prepare your cake tin
    2.
    • Grease it with oil or butter
    • Preheat your oven at 180°C (350°F)

      • OR

      Prepare a deep pot on stove with a stand inside

  2. Mix wet ingredients
    3.
      In another bowl:
    • Beat sugar, egg, and oil until smooth
    • Add milk and vanilla essence
    • Mix again

  3. Combine both
    4.
    • Slowly add dry ingredients to the wet mix
    • Add 1/2 cup hot water or brewed coffee slowly

    (Don’t worry — batter will be thin. That makes it soft!)

  4. Bake or steam
    5.
    • Pour batter into the pan
    • Bake in oven for 30–35 mins

      • OR

    • On stove: Place cake pan in preheated pot, cover with lid, and cook on low heat for 40–45 mins

      • Check with toothpick: if it comes out clean, it’s done!

🍫 Optional Frosting (Simple):

    Mix and heat:
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup icing sugar
  • 1 tbsp milk

    • Spread over the cooled cake.
