☕ Basic Homemade Coffee Recipe (Hot & Frothy)

✅ Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 to 2 tsp instant coffee powder (like Nescafé)
  • 1 to 2 tsp sugar (as you like)
  • 2 tbsp hot water

    👩‍🍳 Step-by-Step (Hand Method – No Machine):

  1. Make the coffee mix (frothy style)
    2.
    • In a cup or bowl, add:
    • Coffee powder + sugar + hot water
    • Mix well with a spoon or whisk it fast for 2–3 minutes
    • The mixture will become light brown and creamy
  2. Heat the milk
    3.
    • In a pan, heat 1 cup milk until it's hot (don’t boil too much)
    • You can also use a milk frother or whisk to make foam
  3. Mix and serve
    4.
    • Pour hot milk into the whipped coffee mix
    • Stir gently and enjoy!
    🧁 Optional:
  • Sprinkle cocoa powder or cinnamon on top
  • Add 1 drop vanilla essence for extra flavor

    🧊 For Cold Coffee:

  1. Add the same coffee-sugar-water mix to a blender
  2. Add cold milk + ice cubes
  3. Blend for 30 seconds
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy chilled!
