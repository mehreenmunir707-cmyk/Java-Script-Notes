🎂 Easy Vanilla Cake Recipe

    ✅ Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter (soft or melted)
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 egg

    🔪 Method (Step-by-step):

  1. Preheat oven

    2. Set your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a cake pan with butter or oil and sprinkle some flour (or use baking paper).

  2. Mix dry ingredients

    3. Mix dry ingredients

    • 1 cup flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 1/4 tsp salt

  3. Mix wet ingredients

    4. In another bowl:

    • Beat 1 egg
    • Add sugar and mix well
    • Add melted butter, milk, and vanilla essence
    • Mix until smooth

  4. Combine wet + dry

    5. Gradually add dry ingredients into the wet mix. Stir gently until the batter is smooth and lump-free.

  5. Pour & bake

    6. Pour the batter into your greased pan. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

  6. Cool & serve

    7. Let the cake cool for 10–15 minutes. You can eat it as-is or top it with:

    • Icing sugar
    • Chocolate syrup
    • Whipped cream or frosting
