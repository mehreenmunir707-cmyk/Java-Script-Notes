🍛 Easy Chicken Biryani Recipe

📝 Ingredients:

    For Rice:
  • 2 cups Basmati rice (washed and soaked for 30 mins)
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf, 2 cloves, 1 cardamom, salt (for boiling)
    For Chicken Masala:
  • 500g chicken (bone or boneless)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tbsp oil
    For Layering:
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves
  • Yellow food color or saffron (optional)
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Fried onions (optional)

🍽 Step-by-Step Method:

    🔹 Step 1: Cook the Rice
  1. Boil 5 cups water with salt, bay leaf, cloves, and cardamom.
  2. Add soaked rice and cook until 70–80% done (not fully soft).
  3. Drain and keep aside.
    🔹 Step 2: Make Chicken Masala
  1. Heat oil in a pot. Fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste; sauté 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken and cook until it changes color.
  4. Add yogurt, tomatoes, salt, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala.
  5. Cook until chicken is tender and masala is thick.
    🔹 Step 3: Layering the Biryani
  1. In a big pot, layer half the rice.
  2. Add all chicken masala over it.
  3. Add rest of the rice on top.
  4. Sprinkle chopped mint, coriander, fried onions, and food color mixed in milk.
  5. Cover tightly with lid or foil.
    🔹 Step 4: Steam (Dum)
  1. Cook on low heat for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

    Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
